Our Privacy Policy



Our Blood Bank realizes how important your personal information is and that is why we are committed to the security and protection of it. We accomplish this by using a wide range of security technologies to protect your information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Some of the ways in which we do this are:



  • We control physical access to the computers that store this information through the use of security personnel and secure rooms.

  • We control electronic access to this information by using access control lists, access policies, as well as firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and other security related systems and devices.

  • When your personal information is transmitted over the Internet we use encryption such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol to protect it.

  • Our website intended for use by the general public and do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
